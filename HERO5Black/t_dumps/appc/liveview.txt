Usage: t appc liveview [command]

    command
         start  starts liveview
         stop   stops liveview
         switch <camera config> switches liveview to new camera config
                                see "t frw pipecfg set" for a list of possible configs
         low_delay <LowDelayMode> <VinVoutSyncDelay>
         video_standard <0/1> switches display module to either NTSC or PAL standard
         clocks show PLL clock configurations
